Engineering roles
Photonic IntegrationPhotonic IC EngineerDetails

Design integrated photonic circuits for OptiHK optical engines, including passive optical components and optoelectronic elements.

Description

  • Design PIC layouts and silicon photonic building blocks.
  • Optimize waveguides, couplers, splitters, filters, and routing structures.
  • Co-design OE elements with packaging, test, and electronic teams.

Requirements

  • PhD in photonics, optical engineering, electrical engineering, physics, or related field.
  • Experience with integrated photonic components or silicon photonics design preferred.
  • Clear documentation and cross-functional communication skills.
ASIC DesignElectronic IC EngineerDetails

Design ASIC chips for OE engines, connecting high-performance electronic design with integrated photonic platforms.

Description

  • Design ASIC architectures and circuit blocks for optoelectronic engines.
  • Work on driver, receiver, control, monitoring, and mixed-signal interfaces.
  • Coordinate PIC-EIC integration with photonic IC and packaging engineers.

Requirements

  • PhD in electrical engineering, microelectronics, IC design, or related field.
  • ASIC, mixed-signal, RF, high-speed, or optoelectronic IC experience preferred.
  • Ability to work through prototype debugging cycles.
Advanced PackagingPackaging EngineerDetails

Carry out advanced packaging design between PIC and EIC platforms, with a focus on thermal dissipation, stress control, and reliable assembly.

Description

  • Design packages that integrate photonic ICs and electronic ICs.
  • Solve thermal, mechanical, alignment, and reliability challenges.
  • Work with suppliers on materials, fixtures, assembly flow, and failure analysis.

Requirements

  • PhD in packaging, mechanical engineering, microelectronics, materials, photonics, or related field.
  • PIC/EIC packaging, thermal design, bonding, or reliability experience preferred.
  • Strong judgment around tolerances, fixtures, metrology, and process control.
OE Engine DesignElectronic Circuit EngineerDetails

Construct circuit-level designs for OE engines, supporting the electrical architecture that drives and validates photonic systems.

Description

  • Design board-level electronics for optoelectronic engines.
  • Develop driver, biasing, monitoring, control, and test circuits.
  • Support bring-up, debugging, and measurement automation.

Requirements

  • Master's degree or above in electrical engineering, electronics, or microelectronics.
  • Analog, mixed-signal, high-speed, low-noise, or optoelectronic circuit experience preferred.
  • Hands-on laboratory debugging skills.
