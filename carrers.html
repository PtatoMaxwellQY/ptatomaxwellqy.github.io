Senior Engineer / Engineer, Electronic IC DesignDetails

Design electronic IC blocks and interfaces for OptiHK optical engines, connecting high-speed electronics with integrated photonic platforms.

Salary

HKD 390k ~ 700k

Description

  • Design ASIC, mixed-signal, driver, receiver, control, and monitoring circuits.
  • Support PIC-EIC integration, prototype bring-up, and measurement debugging.
  • Work with photonic, packaging, board, and test engineers through product cycles.

Requirements

  • Degree in electrical engineering, microelectronics, IC design, or related field.
  • Experience in ASIC, RF, mixed-signal, high-speed, or optoelectronic IC design preferred.
  • Clear documentation and cross-functional engineering communication.
